Privacy Policy - LeadsAutopilot Connect Chrome Extension

Last modified:

1. Introduction

Brookstone Digital Services LLC ("Company," or "we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy, and we are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

This Privacy Policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you use our LeadsAutopilot Connect Chrome Extension (our "Extension") and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

Important: The LeadsAutopilot Connect Chrome Extension is designed with your privacy in mind. No data is automatically sent to any third party. This extension simply displays the values of one of the cookies for you to copy and paste to securely connect your LinkedIn account to LeadsAutopilot servers.

Please read this policy carefully to understand our policies and practices regarding your information and how we will treat it. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, your choice is not to use our Extension. By using our Extension, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Children Under the Age of 16

Our Extension is not intended for children under 16 years of age. No one under age 16 may provide any information through the Extension. We do not knowingly collect Personal Data from children under 16. If you are under 16, do not use or provide any information through our Extension. If we learn we have collected or received Personal Data from a child under 16, we will delete that information. If you believe we might have any information from a child under 16, please contact us at privacy@leadsautopilot.com.

3. Information We Collect About You and How We Collect It

Cookie Access: Our Extension is designed to access and display specific cookie values from LinkedIn to enable you to securely connect your LinkedIn account to our services. These cookie values are only displayed to you and are not automatically transmitted to our servers or any third parties. You must manually copy and paste this information to connect your account.

No Automatic Data Collection

The Extension does not automatically collect or transmit any data. It simply helps you identify and copy certain cookie values that you can then choose to provide to our service to enable account connectivity features.

Information You Choose to Provide

When you choose to connect your LinkedIn account to LeadsAutopilot, you manually copy and paste cookie information displayed by the Extension. This connection allows our server to temporarily access your LinkedIn account to provide the services you've enabled. All connections to our secure server are made over military-grade encryption, and we abide by all data compliance standards.

4. How We Use Your Information

If you choose to provide the cookie information to connect your LinkedIn account, we use this information solely for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate and establish a secure connection between your LinkedIn account and LeadsAutopilot services;
  • To provide the specific LinkedIn-related services you have enabled within your LeadsAutopilot account;
  • To fulfill any other purpose for which you provide it and that you have explicitly enabled through our dashboard.

User Control: You may enable or disable individual services, or disconnect your account entirely at any time via our dashboard, which is separate from the Extension. When you disconnect your account, all cookie information and LinkedIn connection permissions are revoked.

5. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not share, sell, or otherwise disclose your Personal Data for purposes other than those outlined in this Privacy Policy.

The cookie information you provide is used exclusively to establish a secure connection between your LinkedIn account and our services. We do not share this information with any third parties.

6. Data Security

We have implemented measures designed to secure your information from accidental loss and from unauthorized access, use, alteration, and disclosure:

  • All connections to our servers are protected with military-grade encryption;
  • We follow industry best practices for data security and compliance;
  • Cookie information is only processed on your local device and is not automatically transmitted;
  • When you choose to connect your account, the data is transmitted securely to our servers.

The safety and security of your information also depends on you. We recommend that you take care when copying and pasting cookie information and only do so within the LeadsAutopilot website or application.

7. Your Rights and Controls

You have complete control over your connection to LeadsAutopilot:

  • You manually initiate any connection between LinkedIn and LeadsAutopilot;
  • You can enable or disable specific services at any time through our dashboard;
  • You can completely disconnect your LinkedIn account at any time through our dashboard;
  • You can uninstall the Extension at any time to remove it from your browser.
8. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may change this Privacy Policy at any time. It is our policy to post any changes we make to our Privacy Policy on this page. If we make material changes to how we treat your information, we will notify you through a notice within the Extension and/or through our website.
