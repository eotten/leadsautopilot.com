Connect LinkedIn Securely with LeadsAutopilot Connect

Follow these steps to link your LinkedIn account without sharing your password or 2FA code.

1

Download the Extension

First, download the LeadsAutopilot Connect extension ZIP file. You'll need this file in the next steps.

Download Extension ZIP

Once downloaded, unzip the file to a location you can easily find, like your Desktop or Downloads folder.

2

Enable Developer Mode in Chrome

Open Google Chrome, type chrome://extensions/ into your address bar, and press Enter. In the top-right corner of the Extensions page, toggle the "Developer mode" switch on.

3

Load the Extension

With Developer mode enabled, click the "Load unpacked" button that appears on the top-left.

Navigate to the folder where you unzipped the leadsautopilot-connect files in Step 1, select the main folder (the one containing manifest.json), and click "Select Folder".

You should now see the LeadsAutopilot Connect extension listed on your Extensions page.

4

Log in to LinkedIn & Get Token

Make sure you are logged into your LinkedIn account in Chrome. Then, click the puzzle piece icon (Extensions) in your Chrome toolbar and click on "LeadsAutopilot Connect".

The extension will pop up and display your unique Connection Token. Click the "Copy Token" button.

5

Connect Your Account

Paste the copied Connection Token into the designated field in your LeadsAutopilot account settings or provide it to our support team. Once submitted, your LinkedIn account will be securely connected.

Important Security Note

For security reasons, LinkedIn may periodically refresh your session, which invalidates the Connection Token. If this happens, you'll need to repeat Step 4 and Step 5 to generate and submit a new token. We will notify you via email and through your account dashboard if your token needs to be refreshed.
