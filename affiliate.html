Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an active LeadsAutopilot membership to become an affiliate?

Nope. You can promote the platform without paying for it.

Will I earn commissions on affiliates that my referrals bring in?

No. Commissions are single-level only—you earn on the customers you personally refer.

How and when do I get paid?

Our payment provider lets you choose monthly, weekly, daily, or even instant payouts.

What's the cookie window?

30 days. Any purchase a prospect makes within 30 days of clicking your link earns you commission.

Can I promote LeadsAutopilot outside of LinkedIn?

Absolutely. While our ideal users are on LinkedIn, they're active on other platforms too—feel free to reach them wherever they hang out.

Do you provide marketing materials?

Yes. Once you're approved, you'll find banners, product images, and swipe copy in your Whop dashboard—ready to post.
