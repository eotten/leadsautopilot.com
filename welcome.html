Welcome to LeadsAutopilot!

Follow these simple steps to get your account set up and start automating your LinkedIn growth.

1

Reset Your Password

Start by entering your email to reset your password and get access to your account.

Go to:

app.leadsautopilot.com/reset

Enter your email address and follow the link in your email to log in.

2

Update Your Password

Once logged in, go to the settings page to change your password to something secure.

Go to:

app.leadsautopilot.com/settings

Navigate to the settings page and update your password.

3

Add a New Seat

Go to the seats tab and click "Add new seat" to set up your LinkedIn automation.

Go to:

app.leadsautopilot.com/seats

Click the "Add new seat" button to begin connecting your LinkedIn account.

4

Connect Your LinkedIn Account

Enter your LinkedIn credentials or follow the passwordless setup process.

Option A: Enter LinkedIn Credentials

You'll need to provide:

  • LinkedIn email address
  • LinkedIn password
  • 2FA secret key

Important Requirements:

You must have 2FA enabled on your LinkedIn account and set to the secret key option only. Follow the video tutorial below to enable 2FA and get your secret key.

Setup Tutorial Video

Watch our step-by-step video guide to enable 2FA and get your secret key:

Option B: Passwordless Setup

Follow the passwordless steps for a more secure connection method without sharing your password.

Go to:

www.leadsautopilot.com/passwordless

Follow the detailed passwordless setup instructions.

5

Configure Your Seat Settings

Once your seat appears, click on its settings to customize your automation preferences.

Next Steps:
  • Your seat should appear in the seats list
  • Click on the settings icon for your seat
  • Update your automation settings and targeting preferences
  • Configure your messaging templates and connection limits

You're All Set!

Once you've completed these steps, LeadsAutopilot will begin automating your LinkedIn outreach. You should start seeing activity within 24-48 hours.
